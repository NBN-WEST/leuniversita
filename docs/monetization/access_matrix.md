| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Exams** | 1 (Demo) | Unlimited | Specific Course Bundle |

## Tiers Definition

### FREE (Freemium)
- **Goal**: Acquisition & Trust building.
- **Constraints**: Rate limited usage.
- **Access**: `diritto-privato` (or current featured exam).

### PREMIUM (Subscription)
- **Goal**: Revenue & Power Users.
- **Price Point**: €9.99/mo or €49/year (Indicative).
- **Features**: Multi-Exam, Unlimited practice, Advanced adaptation (Strong).

### PILOT (B2B / B2G)
- **Goal**: Institutional Validation & Retention.
- **Model**: Paid by University (Seat-based).
- **Features**: Integration with Prof's slides, Teaching Dashboard.
